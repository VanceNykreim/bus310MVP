Welcome to Our Non-Profit Social Business

We tackle the lack of fresh, affordable, and healthy food by converting vacant lots into gardens and farms.

About Us

Our mission is to create small farm-to-table restaurants using crops from our gardens and farms. We are funded through local government grants and sales of crops to local farmers markets.

Our Projects

We are working on converting vacant lots into productive gardens and farms. Check out our latest projects!

Business Channels

  • Farmers Markets
  • Bottling Workshops
  • On-site Farm Stand

Potential Partners

Local Restaurants

Local restaurants can collaborate by purchasing fresh produce from our gardens, enhancing their farm-to-table offerings and appealing to environmentally and health-conscious customers.

Food Banks

Food banks can partner to distribute surplus produce, increasing access to nutritious food for low-income families and individuals facing food insecurity.

Volunteers

Engaged community members can volunteer their time to help maintain gardens and farms, contributing to sustainability and productivity without significant labor costs.

Donors

Individuals, businesses, and community organizations can donate funds or resources to support community gardens, motivated by the social impact and environmental benefits of our initiative.

Local Government

Municipalities can provide support through grants, land access, or regulatory assistance, interested in promoting healthier lifestyles and reducing food deserts in the community.
